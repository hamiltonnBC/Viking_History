# Viking_History

for my final project in my history of vikings course, I am considering making a 
web application education tool. I am thinking, a map of the area, and a bar at the bottom 
that you can drag from left to right, and as you drag the bar right, the years pass. So you 
can control what year you are looking at. and as you drag the bar along, you can see 
different parts of the map light up, and if you click on them, you can read about what 
happened.
